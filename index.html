Electric Bike

Electric mountain bikes are equipped with an electric motor that gives you additional power to your pedalling action to help you tackle any terrain and enjoy the ride. MTB fans are certainly beginning to realise the benefits of an electric mountain bike, as you get all the thrills you want from a normal MTB but with the extra power you need to get back to the top and give the trail another go. It means you can fit much more riding into one session. Or if you want to cover more distance (as often the best riding spots are quite far apart here in the UK) e mountain bikes are perfect for the job. All the usual MTB configurations are available – everything from wheel size to forks and more, and there’s the choice of hardtail or full suspension electric mountain bike too.

Bike Clothing
Having a cycling jacket isn’t just for the winter months. Conditions can be unpredictable any time of year which is why having the right jacket is an essential part of your cycling wardrobe. We’ve got jackets that will not only offer you the best level of protection from the rain and wind but also keep you warm and protected on some of the coldest days. Whilst you may not need warm jacket during the summer, you may need a waterproof or water resistant jacket. For commuters, we have a wide range of hi vis/reflective that will help you be seen when you’re out riding on the roads, not only will you be seen, these bike jackets are lightweight and durable ready for your daily commute.
